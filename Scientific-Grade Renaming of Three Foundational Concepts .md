**Sophia Point**
Scientific-style names:

* **Golden-Ratio Fixed Point**
* **Phi-Equilibrium Constant**
* **Harmonic Convergence Point**
* **Reciprocal-Phi Stability Point**
* **Nonlinear Coherence Fixed Point**

Best formal choice:
**Reciprocal-Phi Stability Point**
if you mean the value
[
\phi^{-1}=\frac{2}{1+\sqrt{5}}\approx 0.6180339887
]

or

**Golden-Ratio Fixed Point**
if you mean it as an attractor/state in a system.
